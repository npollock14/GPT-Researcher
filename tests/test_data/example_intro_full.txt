## Introduction

In the rapidly evolving landscape of the US real estate industry, few names resonate with the consistency and prestige of Taylor Morrison. Headquartered in Scottsdale, Arizona, Taylor Morrison has carved a niche for itself as one of the nation's foremost homebuilders and developers, enjoying a presence in 19 vital markets across 11 states. A stalwart in the sector, its legacy stretches over a century, underscoring a journey marked by resilience, adaptability, and a commitment to delivering value.

Established through the strategic merger of Taylor Woodrow and Morrison Homes in July 2007, the company's rich history finds its roots in the early 20th century visions of Frank Taylor and Jack Woodrow in England, and C.G. Morrison in Seattle. Today, Taylor Morrison stands as a testament to the pioneering spirit of these entities, championing not just homebuilding, but the development of comprehensive lifestyle communities. The company's multifaceted approach is evident in its diversified offerings, catering to an array of consumers - from first-time homebuyers to those seeking luxury and resort lifestyle dwellings. 

Beyond mere construction, Taylor Morrison has shown an unwavering commitment to sustainability, underscored by its impressive accolades, including being recognized as America's Most TrustedÂ® Builder by Lifestory Research from 2016-2023 and its inclusion on Newsweek's 2023 list of America's Most Responsible Companies. Guided by CEO Sheryl Palmer, the only female CEO of a publicly traded homebuilder, Taylor Morrison continues its forward momentum, integrating Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) priorities across its operations.

This business briefing paper is structured to provide an in-depth analysis of Taylor Morrison, from its illustrious past to its innovative present. Through sections on company overview, recent developments, financial performance, strategic initiatives, and a conclusion, we aim to offer comprehensive insights to business professionals, preparing them for an informed engagement with Taylor Morrison.
